Why Steam Download Speed Is Slow

Wrong Download Region

Steam servers can be busy. Switching regions often improves download speed.

Disk Bottlenecks

Slow drives can limit Steam download speed because Steam installs while downloading.

Wi-Fi Instability

Wireless signal drops cause lower average download speed.

Bandwidth Competition

Other devices, streams, or updates can reduce available download speed.

Steam Download Speed Fixes

Change Download Region

Steam Settings ? Downloads ? choose a nearby region with lower traffic.

Remove Download Limits

Make sure Steam is not capped by a speed limit in its settings.

Switch to Ethernet

Use a wired connection to stabilize download speed.

Pause + Resume

Restarting the download can reconnect you to a better server.

Steam Speed Units Explained

Steam often shows download speed in MB/s, while most ISPs advertise Mbps. Divide Mbps by 8 to estimate MB/s.
