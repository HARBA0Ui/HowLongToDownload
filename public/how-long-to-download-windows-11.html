Tips for a Faster Windows 11 Download

Use Ethernet

Wired connections keep download speed stable for large ISO files.

Pause Other Traffic

Stop streaming and cloud backups during the Windows 11 download.

Download Off-Peak

Late night or early morning often delivers higher download speed.

Check Storage Space

Make sure there is enough disk space so the download does not stall.
