Tips to Speed Up Uploads

Use Ethernet

Wired connections are more stable and avoid Wi-Fi interference.

Pause Heavy Apps

Stop cloud syncs or video calls that compete for upstream bandwidth.

Schedule Off-Peak

Upload when the network is quiet to reduce congestion.

Enable Resume

Use tools that support resumable uploads in case of interruptions.
