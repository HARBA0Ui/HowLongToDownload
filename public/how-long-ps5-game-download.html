Tips to Speed Up PS5 Downloads

1) Use Wired Connection

Connect PS5 via Ethernet cable instead of Wi-Fi. Ethernet provides 2-3x faster, more stable speeds and eliminates interference.

2) Minimize Network Activity

Close other apps using bandwidth (streaming, updates, other devices). Downloads are significantly faster when they have the full pipe.

3) Download During Off-Peak Hours

Late night and early morning typically have less network congestion. Start large downloads before bed for completion by morning.

4) Keep PS5 Ventilated

Ensure proper airflow around your PS5. Overheating can throttle performance, including download speeds.

5) Check for Interference

If on Wi-Fi, move closer to your router and away from microwaves, cordless phones, and other 2.4GHz devices.

6) Restart Your Router

Sometimes a simple restart improves connection stability and speeds. Power cycle for 30 seconds before downloading.
